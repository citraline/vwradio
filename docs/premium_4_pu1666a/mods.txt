Must have:
x    BUS(to radio)
x    CLK(to radio)
x    DAT(to radio)
x    STB(to radio)
x    BUS(to faceplate)
x    CLK(to faceplate)
x    DAT(to faceplate)
x    STB(to faceplate)
x    LOF(to radio+faceplate)
x    !POWER_EJECT
x    EJECT
x    TAPE CLOCK
x    TAPE DATA
x    TAPE ENABLE
x    TAPE SWITCH

Header Pinout
-------------

                 24 pin
                +------+
           GND  | o  o |  Unswitched +14V
           LOF  | o  o |  Unswitched +5V
    BUS(radio)  | o  o |  BUS(face)
    CLK(radio)  | o  o |  CLK(face)
    DAT(radio)  | o  o |  DAT(face)
    STB(radio)    o  o |  STB(face)
  !POWER_EJECT  | o  o |  EJECT
      TAPE_CLK  | o  o |  TAPE_DAT
      TAPE_ENA  | o  o |  TAPE_SWI
                | o  o |
                | o  o |
           GND  | o  o |  Unswitched +14V
                +------+
